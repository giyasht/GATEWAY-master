About Us

An app that simplifies life for everyone in a gated community, from residents and management committee members to security guards and facility managers. Packed with features, it reduces many hassles—authorising entry of delivery executives, paying maintenance bills, raising a ticket to the facility manager—to a single click.

Features

Announcements
The admin can post important announcements and notices which are directly visible on the user’s home page.
Complaints
A user can raise a complaint regarding the amenities and maintenance. The admin is responsible for looking in it and close it once it is solved.
Visitor Log
The user can keep a track of visitors, deliverymen, relatives visiting the house as well as allowing or denying entry.
Vehicle Log
Keep track of all the vehicles registered under a flat and the designated parking slots.
Event calendar
All the events and important dates can be accessed through the calendar so that the user doesn’t miss any deadlines or events.
Contacts
All the important contacts can be found here so that all the services can be accessed easily.
